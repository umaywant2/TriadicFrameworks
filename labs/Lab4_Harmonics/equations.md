# Quantum Harmonics – Equations & Interpretations

## 🔁 Harmonic Oscillator Energy Levels

$$
E_n = \left(n + \frac{1}{2}\right)\hbar \omega
$$

- \( n \): Quantum number (0, 1, 2, ...)
- \( \omega \): Angular frequency
- \( \hbar \): Reduced Planck constant
